About Dominque Hernandez

In November 2012, at the young age of 16, Dominique Marie Hernandez lost her life in a tragic automobile accident. She was a beautiful & talented young softball player and loved spending her free time volunteering at organizations that were close to her heart.

Dominique was such an all-round athelete but Softball was definitely her game. She started playing softball at such a young age and right off the bat, her Daddy and Grandpa knew Dominique was an All-Star.

After Dominique's accident, the family was thinking of a way to keep Dominique's memory alive. Well, there's no other way than to have a Softball Tournament in her honor (memory).

If you knew Nikki, you knew she loved to be around her family and friends. Making new friends, being outside and LOVED PLAYING BALL.

We Love you Dominique.

R.I.P
